Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our signage solutions

How long does it take to create a custom sign?

Our standard turnaround time for custom signs ranges from 7-14 business days depending on complexity, materials, and current production volume. For urgent needs, we also offer expedited services that can deliver in as little as 3-5 business days for an additional fee.

What materials do you use for your signage?

We work with a wide range of premium materials including acrylic, aluminum, brass, bronze, stainless steel, wood, and various composites. Our team will recommend the best material based on your specific requirements, location (indoor/outdoor), budget, and aesthetic preferences.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we offer professional installation services for all our signage products. Our experienced installation team ensures your sign is securely and properly mounted according to local regulations. For clients outside our service area, we provide detailed installation instructions and can recommend trusted local installers.
